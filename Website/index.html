How it works

Built different, by design.

Three principles that make NOBS possible — and that no big-tech AI app can match.

🧠

On-Device AI

iPhone 15 Pro and iPhone 16+ run NOBS entirely through Apple Intelligence. Inference never leaves your chip. Not your carrier. Not Apple's servers. Not ours.

🏠

Home Server

Older hardware? NOBS Server runs on your own machine at home — a Mac mini, a NUC, or a Raspberry Pi. You own the hardware. You own the model. You own the data.

🔒

Your data stays home

No OpenAI. No Google. No Anthropic. No AWS. Every conversation, memory, and task lives on a device you physically control. Full stop.

Features

Everything you need. Nothing you don't.

NOBS integrates the parts of your life that matter most — and keeps them yours.

🧩

Memories

Capture and recall anything. Your AI learns you over time without ever syncing to a cloud service.

✅

Tasks

Smart task management with AI context. Your assistant understands your priorities — not just your to-do list.

💡

HomeKit

Control your home with natural language. "Turn off the living room after the movie" just works.

Health

AI that knows your health context. On-device only — Apple Health data never leaves your phone.

🎙️

Voice

Encrypted voice conversations with your AI. Transcription and understanding happen on your hardware.
