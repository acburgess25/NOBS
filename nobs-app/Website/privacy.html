Privacy Policy

Your data stays yours. Period.

Last updated: May 2025

NOBS is built on a single premise: your personal AI assistant should never need to see your data. All AI processing happens on your device or on your own private server. Nothing leaves your control.

Information We Collect

NOBS collects no personal data on our servers. When you grant permissions, all data stays on your device:

  • Microphone — voice input for AI conversations, processed locally
  • HomeKit — smart home control, never transmitted externally
  • Reminders & Calendar — scheduling assistance, stored on-device
  • Contacts — people-aware responses, read locally only
  • Health — wellness insights, never leaves your device
  • Location — context-aware responses, not logged or stored
  • Photos — image analysis only when you explicitly share a photo

All permissions are optional. NOBS functions without them.

What We Don't Do

  • We do not send your conversations to any cloud service
  • We do not collect analytics, usage data, or behavioral signals
  • We do not sell or share your data with any third party
  • We do not require account creation
  • We do not use advertising or tracking SDKs

How AI Processing Works

NOBS runs AI models on-device using Apple's Neural Engine or connects to your own private local server (e.g. on your home network). No Anthropic, OpenAI, or other third-party AI API is called. Your conversations never touch anyone else's infrastructure.

Data Storage

Conversation history and preferences are stored in your device's local storage (Core Data / iCloud if you enable it). iCloud sync, if used, is protected by Apple's end-to-end encryption.

In-App Purchases

NOBS Pro is purchased through Apple's App Store. We never see or handle your payment information — all transactions are handled entirely by Apple.

Children

NOBS is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Significant changes will be noted in the App Store release notes. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

Questions about privacy? We're reachable at support@nobsdash.com
