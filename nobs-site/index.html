Your personal health data stays securely on your own hardware.

Prescriptions, wellness goals, and dietary preferences are encrypted on your local database. NOBS stores absolutely no copies.

Apple Security Native

Secured behind Face ID / Touch ID, utilizing Apple's CryptoKit, Keychain, and iOS App Sandbox limits for maximum data isolation.
