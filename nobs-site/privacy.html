Privacy Policy

NOBS Privacy

NOBS is designed around local-first operation, fully encrypted health logs, and absolute control over your personal medical, wellness, and dietary data.

Local-First Health Data

NOBS processes all health telemetry — including medication checkmarks, screen time metrics, and food logs — entirely on your own Apple device. No medical history is ever transmitted to, or stored on, external cloud servers.

Apple Health & HealthKit

NOBS integrates directly with Apple HealthKit. NOBS reads your health metrics and logs medication dose events locally. This data is protected by iOS system sandboxing and remains encrypted on-device.

Digital Wellness & Screen Time

NOBS uses local device APIs to monitor active screen usage and provide eye-rest prompts (the clinical 20-20-20 rule). NOBS does not track the specific apps you use, websites you visit, or your location.

Hosted AI & Compute

All chat-based health advice, diet coaching, and macro planning runs on-device using Apple Intelligence. If you choose to opt-in to alex's hosted beta server (Tank) for more complex tasks, data is processed statelessly and immediately deleted.

Data Control

Your health records belong to you. You can export all logged information in standard JSON format, or wipe the local database completely in one tap from the settings panel.
