# NOBS Brand Direction

NOBS should feel friendly, clean, secure, and Apple-native.

## Core Feeling

- Friendly: warm language, rounded system type, clear human benefits.
- Clean: generous spacing, fewer words, calm surfaces, no noisy decoration.
- Secure: shield mark, explicit permission language, visible privacy posture.
- Alive: motion lines, signal paths, and subtle breathing states instead of loud
  effects.

## Logo

The current logo is a simple `NOBS` wordmark with a single signal dot. It should
read as:

- Friendly.
- Modern.
- Clean.
- Secure without feeling like a cybersecurity company.
- Precise enough for Apple users and soft enough for families.

## Motion

Use motion to show coordination:

- Lines connect people, devices, and approval decisions.
- Cards lift gently.
- Signals breathe softly.
- Buttons respond quickly.

Avoid random glow balls, noisy gradients, heavy particles, shields, lock icons,
robot faces, or anything that feels like a crypto/security dashboard.
